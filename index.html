How the Classification Works

ABC — How Valuable Is This Part?

A = Top 70% of spend — Manage tightly, never allow a stockout
B = Next 20% of spend — Monitor regularly
C = Bottom 10% of spend — Light-touch management

XYZ — How Predictable Is Demand?

X = Steady demand — Easy to forecast accurately
Y = Variable demand — Needs closer attention
Z = Sporadic demand — Unpredictable, needs special methods
