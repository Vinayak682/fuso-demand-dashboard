Segmentation-Based Forecasting Engine

METHOD BY SEGMENT · SBA · CROSTON · CAUSAL
AX / BX Weighted Moving Average

Stable parts with CV < 0.10. High value, predictable demand. Continuous automated replenishment. Min-max with tight control bands. Recent months weighted 3×.

Forecast = Σ(wᵢ × Dᵢ) / Σwᵢ
w₁=3, w₂=2, w₃=1 (recent → older)
AY / AZ Exponential Smoothing

High-value parts with trend (fleet growth, new model launches). Adaptive alpha responds to MEA market signals. Monthly demand review with consensus override.

Fₜ = α × Dₜ₋₁ + (1 − α) × Fₜ₋₁
α = 0.3 (balanced responsiveness)
BZ / CZ SBA — Syntetos-Boylan

Intermittent demand parts with zero-demand months. SBA corrects Croston's systemic over-forecasting bias. Separates demand size from demand interval.

F_SBA = (1 − α/2) × (Ẑ / p̂)
Ẑ = demand size · p̂ = demand interval
eCanter EV Causal / Telematics-Driven

Electric FUSO eCanter parts triggered by telematics. Battery SoH below 85% or odometer above 150k km auto-generates VOR (Vehicle Off Road) demand signals.

IF(mileage > 150k AND SoH < 85%)
→ URGENT VOR demand trigger
